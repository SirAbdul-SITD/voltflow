APP TITLE (max 50 chars):
VoltFlow: Wire Connect Puzzle

SHORT DESCRIPTION (max 80 chars):
Draw glowing wires, link every power node and fill the board. 150 levels!

FULL DESCRIPTION:
⚡ DRAW. LINK. ENERGIZE. ⚡

VoltFlow is a beautifully minimal wire-drawing puzzle. Drag your finger to draw glowing neon wires between matching power nodes — but be careful: wires can never cross, and every single cell of the board must be energized to win!

🔌 HOW TO PLAY
• Touch a power node and drag to draw its wire
• Connect it to the matching colored node
• Wires cannot cross each other
• Fill 100% of the board to complete the level

✨ FEATURES
• 150 handcrafted levels across 3 difficulties
• Easy 5×5, Medium 6×6 and Hard 7×7 boards
• Smooth one-finger drawing controls
• Stunning neon glow visuals
• Relaxing ambient soundtrack
• 3-star rating system — solve in fewer moves for more stars
• Haptic feedback
• Works fully offline — no internet needed
• No ads, no in-app purchases, no data collection

🧠 EASY TO LEARN, HARD TO MASTER
Early levels teach you the flow. Later boards demand real planning — one wrong wire and the board locks up. Can you 3-star all 150?
